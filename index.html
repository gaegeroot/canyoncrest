OUR APPROACH

The land is not a backdrop.

It is the architecture.

Free estimates · No pressure · Tucson-based team

High desert ground holds millions of years of intention — strata, drainage, light. Most landscaping fights that ground into submission. We don't. Canyon Crest designs from the topography outward: every terrace follows an existing grade, every stone is selected for how it already sits in the land, and every plant is native to the mile you live on.

Whether you're refreshing a front yard or transforming a full property, you'll work directly with our Tucson-based team from the first visit to the final stone. No sales reps, no subcontracted strangers.

HOW WE WORK

Simple from start to finish.

Four straightforward steps — from the first conversation to a yard you'll use every day.

01

Free On-Site Estimate

We come to you. No showroom, no obligation — just a walkthrough of your property and an honest conversation about what's possible and what it costs.

02

Design & Plan

We develop a design tailored to your space, budget, and the desert's natural light and drainage. You'll see the full plan before a shovel touches the ground.

03

Skilled Installation

Our own crew handles every step — stonework, planting, irrigation, and hardscape — with the same care on a $5,000 job as on a $50,000 one.

04

Ongoing Support

We stand behind our work. Any plant we install that fails from our error is replaced, with no expiration date. Seasonal stewardship programs available.
